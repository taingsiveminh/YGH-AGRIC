About Us

YGH & VN AGRIC (CAMBODIA) CO., LTD. is dedicated to providing the freshest and highest quality fruits from our Cambodian farms to customers around the world.

Our commitment to sustainable farming practices ensures that every product is not only delicious but also grown with care for the environment. We specialize in longan and durian, bringing the unique and vibrant flavors of our region to your table.
